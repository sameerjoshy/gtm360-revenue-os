# GTM 360 Website Content & Structure

## PAGE 1 — HOME
**Brand:** GTM360 — Revenue OS

### A. GLOBAL LAYOUT & NAVIGATION (MANDATORY)
**Header / Top Menu (Desktop)**
Structure: `[ GTM360 logo ] Problems We Solve | How We Work | Cases & Insights | Partners | About | [ Contact ]`
*   Logo: left-aligned
*   Navigation: right-aligned
*   “Contact”: text button or subtle outline (not filled)
*   Sticky: Only after first scroll, reduces height by ~30%
*   NO: Sidebar, icons, animation

**Mobile Navigation**
*   Hamburger menu
*   Order: Problems, How We Work, Cases, About, Partners, Contact

### B. COLOR SCHEMA (LOCKED)
*   **Primary:** Deep Indigo / Blue (from logo), White (dominant bg)
*   **Secondary:** Soft Neutral Grey (#F6F7F9), Charcoal / Near-black (body)
*   **Accent:** Muted violet/blue gradient (ONLY for diagrams/dividers)
*   **Avoid:** Bright colors, groweth green, dashboard styles.

### C. TYPOGRAPHY
*   Headlines: Modern sans-serif, medium-to-bold.
*   Body: Neutral sans-serif, high readability.
*   Tone: Calm, architectural, senior.
